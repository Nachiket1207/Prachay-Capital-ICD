03 — Campaign Flow
The ICD Lead Funnel
1
💼 LinkedIn + Google
Awareness — Enter the CFO's World
Decision-makers see Prachay for the first time via targeted content, search ads, or peer recommendations at industry events.
◆LinkedIn Sponsored Post: "Your corporate treasury deserves better than 5%. See how Prachay ICD delivers more." — targeted to CFO title
◆Google Search: "inter corporate deposit rates India" → Prachay ICD landing page with rate calculator
◆YouTube Pre-roll on Business Standard / ET Now videos: 15s hook "Short-term surplus? Earn more with Prachay ICD"
◆Founder article on LinkedIn: "The ₹10,000 Crore opportunity India's CFOs are missing in their treasury"
2
📧 Email + LinkedIn
Interest — Hook with Insight
The CFO or finance head engages — clicks an ad, reads an article, or visits the website. Interest is captured through valuable content, not a hard sell.
◆LinkedIn article: "3 ways Indian CFOs are beating liquid fund returns in 2026 — legally" — gated download captures email
◆Landing page ICD Rate Calculator: "Enter your surplus amount and tenure — see your daily earnings" → form capture
◆Webinar registration: "Optimising Corporate Treasury Returns: ICD vs Liquid Funds vs FD" — CFO-targeted invite
◆WhatsApp Click-to-Chat: "Type ICD to receive our corporate rate card and product note instantly"
3
💬 Email Drip + WhatsApp
Nurture — Build Confidence
Corporate decision-makers require more trust-building than retail investors. A structured drip educates, de-risks, and keeps Prachay top-of-mind.
◆Day 1 Email: "Welcome + ICD Product Note + Prachay NBFC credentials (RBI registration, CRISIL rating, track record)"
◆Day 3 WhatsApp: 2-min video — "How ICD works at Prachay — step by step, from deposit to maturity"
◆Day 5 Email: "Case Study — How a Pune manufacturing company deployed ₹3Cr surplus at [X]% for 180 days"
◆Day 8 LinkedIn Retargeting: CRISIL-rated, RBI-registered trust signals — for compliance-conscious finance teams
◆Day 11 WhatsApp: "You're invited — exclusive CFO Breakfast hosted by Prachay leadership next Friday"
4
📞 RM + In-Person
Qualify — Meet the Decision-Maker
RM engages directly. ICD is a high-ticket B2B product — qualification requires a real conversation, not just a form submission.
◆RM calls within 24 hours of any lead form submission — asks 4 qualification questions: Company size, surplus amount, tenure, decision timeline
◆Personalized ICD proposal prepared: "For ₹[X]Cr over [Y] months at Prachay, here is your projected income vs alternatives"
◆Hot leads: 1:1 meeting at Prachay office (14th floor, Next Gen Avenue) or prospect's corporate office
◆CA partner introduced if prospect prefers CA-routed transaction — removes friction for compliance-sensitive clients
5
⚡ Legal + Ops
Convert — Execute & Onboard
Smooth documentation, board resolution support, and fast turnaround convert a qualified corporate into a confirmed ICD depositor.
◆ICD Agreement drafted and shared within 48 hours of verbal confirmation — legal team on standby
◆Prachay ops team assists with board resolution template (required under Companies Act Section 186)
◆Funds received → Welcome kit dispatched: signed agreement, interest schedule, RM contact details
◆Interest credited on schedule with automated alert to CFO/Finance head on each payment date
6
🔄 Renewal + Referral
Retain & Multiply — Renew and Grow
A corporate that renews ICD is worth 10x a one-time deposit. Systematic relationship management and referral requests build compounding pipeline.
◆30-day pre-maturity renewal offer: WhatsApp + Email + RM call — higher rate for loyal depositors
◆Quarterly RM check-in call with CFO — update on Prachay business health and new rate options
◆Referral ask post-first interest payment: "Do you know another CFO/business owner who'd benefit from Prachay ICD?"
◆Cross-sell: ICD depositors introduced to Prachay Bonds (NCD) for their personal/promoter portfolio
